    • ABOUT AESPA

    aespa (에스파; also stylized æspa; debut November 17, 2020) is a four-member South Korean girl group under SM Entertainment. aespa debuted on November 17, 2020, with the single Black Mamba. The 4 members of aespa are Karina, Winter, Giselle, and Ningning. The meaning of the name aespa comes from the words æ (which means Avatar X Experience) and the word Aspect (which represents two sides). Aespa wants to show its innovative and diverse activities through its avatar self. Recently, aespa come back with their fresh and cool new music titled BETTER THINGS.
